About Us

The Taj Indian Restaurant prides itself in serving authentic Indian Cuisine. Using recipes perfected by the royal chefs of Rajas and Maharajas, the rich heritage of India is translated into mouthwatering dining experience. The exotic and aromatic cuisine is not a product of isolation as it actually is an infusion of many different cultures and nationalities. The Moguls, who were the direct decedents of the Genghis Khan, as well as Persian, British, and Portuguese all had their influence on this cuisine. For example the Indian dish Vindaloo is derived from the Portuguese dish "Carne de Vinha d' Alhos" while the Chicken Tikka Masala was made famous by the British. So come enjoy the rich heritage of India and its cuisine with us at the Taj Indian Restuarat
